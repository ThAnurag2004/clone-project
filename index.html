Overview

India, officially known as the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area, the most populous country, and the largest democracy in the world.

Culture

India is known for its diverse culture, languages, religions, and festivals. Major religions such as Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism originated here. Popular festivals include:

  • Diwali – Festival of Lights
  • Holi – Festival of Colors
  • Eid – Celebrated by Muslims
  • Christmas – Celebrated by Christians

Geography

India is bordered by:

  1. Pakistan to the northwest
  2. China and Nepal to the north
  3. Bhutan to the northeast
  4. Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east

It has a vast coastline along the Indian Ocean, Bay of Bengal, and Arabian Sea.
